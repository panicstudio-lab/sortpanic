Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

At Sort Panic, your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect

  • Local Game Data: Your high scores and level progress are stored locally on your device.
  • Monthly Challenge: The app fetches the current monthly challenge from our server.
  • No Personal Information: We don't collect names, emails, or any personally identifiable information.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your data
  • We don't track you across websites
  • We don't require accounts or sign-ins
  • We don't use analytics or advertising trackers

Data Storage

All your game data (scores, unlocked levels) is stored locally on your device. We don't have access to it.

Monthly Challenge

The app fetches a small JSON file from sortpanic.com to display the current monthly challenge. This request does not include any personal information.

Third-Party Services

We use GitHub Pages to host our website and monthly challenge file. GitHub may collect standard web server logs (IP address, browser type) as described in their privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Sort Panic is suitable for all ages. We don't knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date will reflect any changes.

Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Contact us at: Contact Page

TL;DR: We don't collect your data. Your scores stay on your device. No accounts needed. That's it.
