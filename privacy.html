Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

At Sort Panic, we believe in transparency. This policy explains exactly what data we collect (spoiler: almost nothing) and how we use it.

The Short Version: We don't collect personal data. Your game progress stays on your device. No tracking, no ads (for now), no accounts required.

Do You Collect Personal Data?

No. We don't collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information. Sort Panic doesn't require accounts, emails, names, or any personal details.

What Data Does the App Store?

All game data is stored locally on your device:

  • High scores: Saved on your device only
  • Unlocked levels: Saved on your device only
  • Game preferences: Saved on your device only

We cannot access this data. If you uninstall the app, all data is deleted.

Do You Use Cookies or Analytics?

Website: Our website (sortpanic.com) uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This helps us improve the website experience.

What Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages you visit on our website
  • How long you stay on each page
  • Where you came from (e.g., search engines, social media)
  • Approximate location (city/country level)
  • Device type (mobile/desktop/tablet)

What we do NOT collect:

  • Your name or email address
  • Personal information
  • Precise GPS location

The app itself: The Sort Panic app does NOT use analytics or tracking. Your gameplay is completely private.

You can opt out of Google Analytics using browser extensions like uBlock Origin or by adjusting your browser settings.

What About the Monthly Challenge?

The app fetches a small configuration file from sortpanic.com/monthly-challenge.json to display the current month's challenge. This request does not include any personal information or tracking data.

Our web host (GitHub Pages) may log standard server information (IP address, timestamp) as described in GitHub's Privacy Policy.

Will You Show Ads?

Not currently. Sort Panic has no ads right now.

If we add ads in the future (for example, via AdMob), ad networks may collect data like:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (for regional ads)
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, views)

If this happens, we will update this policy first and give users the option to support us through other means (like a one-time purchase to remove ads).

Third-Party Services

Sort Panic may use these third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Our website uses Google Analytics to understand visitor behavior. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages: Hosts our website and monthly challenge file. See GitHub's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Store: If you download from the Play Store, Google's policies apply. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Sort Panic is suitable for all ages. We don't knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Significant changes will be announced in the app or on our website.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to request, delete, or export. Your data stays on your device, and you control it completely.
